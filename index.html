Our Mission Statement
Our mission at Girls Who Code QC is to provide a safe and inclusive environment for women and nonbinary students, primarily for those pursuing a career in computer science. We hope that in doing so, the gender gap in fields of STEM will begin to diminish, as more underrepresented groups of people feel more confident in their abilities and feel inclined to pursue a STEM career. We intend on providing our members with resources and opportunities so that they can build on both their technical skills and confidence.












Why Girls Who Code?
The gender disparity in Computer Science, and fields alike, are undeniable. When you walk into an introductory Computer Science course, you'll notice that the men outnumber the women.
